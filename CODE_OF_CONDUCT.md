# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project
a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the research and the community
- Showing empathy towards other contributors

Examples of unacceptable behaviour include:

- Harassment, trolling, or insulting/derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional or academic setting

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be
reported to the project team via the contact route in `docs/SUPPORT.md`.
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
